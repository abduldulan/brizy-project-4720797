General Corporate and Commercial

01

We cover a broad range of corporate and business affairs and transactions. Our general corporate and commercial team provides the fundamental legal services and support which every business needs, in terms of corporate secretarial services, general legal consultancy, contract drafting and review, employment, regulatory advice and compliance, corporate governance, corporate structuring, restructuring and reorganization exercises, immigration permit and advice.

We assist our clients in handling all types of legal services related to the daily operation of business and legal issues, diverse corporate legal affairs, formation of legal entities in Indonesia, corporate transactional matters, commercial contracts and documentation. We also advise on and draft contracts for a wide spectrum of businesses, ranging from the daily operational matters to other commercial transactions as well as for the settlement of dispute. 

Employment

02

We assist both individuals and companies in handling their employment matters and providing advisory work on the complex rules governing the employment relationships. Besides drafting employment contracs and confidentiality agreements, we also review existing employment contracts, human resources and personnel policies and practices, employee handbooks to ensure that they are all in compliance with applicable local regulations. 

We also offer legal advice on the employment implications of mergers and acquisitions and the rights and benefits available to employees upon termination. In the event of disputes, our firm also represents clients in settlement negotiations and appear before the Indonesian Industrial Relations Courts. We also arrange work permits, visas, residency permits and related governmental approvals and filings. 

Corporate Mergers & Acquisitions

03

Corporate acquisitions and mergers have always been an important strategic option for companies, financial institutions and private equity investors. Our clients rely on our advice to help understand and assess these options and execute their most complex transactions. 

We assist our clients from pre-transaction planning and structuring through due diligence, negotiating and drafting definitive documentation, financing, incorporation of local subsidiaries and the procurement of local regulatory approvals, closing and resolution of post-closing adjustments and other post-closing issues.

Our merger and acquisition team strive to protect the value of our clients' transactions while at the same time making certain that we provide practical solutions to the issues that arise in the course of our representation. We understand that our clients want to work with lawyers who can not only identify important risks, but can also negotiate to minimize and mitigate those risks while maximizing the realization of the clients' goals and objectives. 

Foreign Direct Investment

04

In today’s globalized economy, businesses regularly seek ways to boost their performance, expand and gain access to local and emerging markets. Yeo & Co provides experienced legal representation for international clients doing business in Indonesia and domestic clients doing business internationally. 

Specifically for Asia-based companies, we have a team of attorneys who are fluent in Chinese and Taiwanese, and have extensive knowledge of the cultural and legal systems that affect Chinese investment in Indonesia, as well as understand the unique needs of Asia-based clients.

We advise companies doing business in Asia with regard to foreign direct investment in Asia; strategic alliances; regulatory and litigation matters; bank lending and finance; and international trade, including import/export matters and related issues.

We usually represent companies in a broad range of industries that include agriculture, energy, high technology, natural resources, transportation, financial technology, entertainment, logistics, manufacturing, real estate, and consumer goods. We are also experienced in the purchase, sale, and combination of a variety of public and privately-owned businesses.
