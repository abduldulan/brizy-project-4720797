Yeo&Co is a boutique practice firm with specialised expertise in various areas of laws such as foreign investment, mergers & acquisitions, employment, intellectual property, Information Technology, Telecommunication and E-commerce, civil and commercial litigations.

Yeo&Co was founded as an entrepreneurial, client-focused law firm that provides sophisticated legal services for corporations, groups and individuals. We are dedicated to representing our clients with the highest level of skill, advocacy, integrity, responsiveness and professionalism in the matters with which we are entrusted. Our primary emphasis is on understanding and strategically accomplishing the business and legal goals of our clients.

Yeo&Co serves a wide array of clients, from small and medium enterprises to multinational corporations and public listed companies, in addition to private individuals. We represent key players from the region and the international arena including China and Asean countries in the different business sectors​.

Our vision is to be a trusted partner with valuable long-term relationship, to our clients.

Our mission is to build strong client relationships and deliver the highgest quality of services to help our clients achieve their full potential in business and make distinctive, lasting and substantial improvements in their performance while holding strong to our core values.

Our Core Values

At Yeo & Co, our core values are at the heart of our culture and guide our actions and decisions. It defines who we are, how we act, what we believe in and what we stand for. Our team strives to live out our vision, mission and core values each day. The core values which we strive to deliver to our clients on every day as follows:

01

Ethics & Integrity

We hold each other to maintaining the highest of ethical and moral standards. We strive to communicate openly, honestly and frequently. We believe a reputation is built on being reliable. We have high expectations of our role and believe that being reliable, honest and professional allows the formation of lasting business relationships.

We are fully committed and dedicated to our clients. We deliver consistent quality and exceptional services to our clients. Clients can count on us as a reliable partner that provides predictable results to benefit clients’ business. We are dedicated to satisfying our clients’ needs and engage willingly as partners in their success. We are committed to consistently delivering value and reliability to our clients.

02

Professionalism

Professionalism is the culmination of competence, knowledge, resourcefulness, quality, attitude, and cooperation related to how we provide our professional services. Yeo & Co strives to provide its clients with not only a superb finished product but also with an exceptional experience and the highest professional standards. We put client’s interests ahead of the firm’s and manage client and firm resources cost-effectively. While it is important to be recognized for quality and knowledge, it is also essential to leave our clients with a positive view of our entire firm. Demonstration of a positive attitude, competence, and cooperation on all levels creates an immeasurable positive impact when it comes to a client choosing to return to the firm or recommending our services to another potential client, thus leading to growth. 

03

Respect

We treat everyone with respect at all times. We believe in fostering a culture of respect for each other and accountability for our actions. We encourage entrepreneurial client service and individualism balanced with collaboration to ensure the best outcomes possible. It is important to us that our clients, employees, and partners understand that they are working for mutual benefit, through times of success and difficulty.

We respect our clients by recognizing their needs and objectives. We respect our own colleagues by recognizing their strengths, contribution and personal circumstances. We respect our competitors because together we create an environment that pushes us to constantly excel.

04

Commitment to Client Success

We believe that we will be successful if our clients are successful. Success can be defined in many ways, but for us, making sure that our clients achieve success is our highest priority.
