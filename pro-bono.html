PRO BONO

Our lawyers are working pro bono to ensure access to justice, promote the rule of law, and provide legal services to those unable to afford a lawyer. Everyone at Yeo & Co plays a role in our pro bono practice. We encourage our lawyers to spend time each year providing free legal services to individuals who cannot otherwise afford a lawyer and organizations that qualify for pro bono assistance

We work hard to achieve the same levels of excellence in our pro bono and community investment work as in everything else we do. For more information about the Pro Bono Program, please contact us at info@yeoandco.com.
